Our Story

Conner's Side:

In February, I swiped up on a GORGEOUS girl in mutual. I knew I had to send a killer message to get her attention. I settled on 'Hey.' Smooth right?

A few months of chatting back and forth later, we were on our first date to Salem Days. I had planned a night picnic with candles and Costa Vida. I was going to pull out all the stops and impress this girl. Fast forward to me switching on the battery powered candles, and nothing happening. I had forgotten the batteries in the battery powered candles. She had to have been swooning so hard. There was this beautiful girl, who made me nervous just to look at her, and I had forgot a major detail that would help make the night. Somehow, she forgave me for that and I convinced her to go on several more dates with me.

Sitting at Temple Square Conference Center, watching the sunset holding her in my arms, I realized I could do that forever. A few weeks later sitting in her car at Salt Lake Central Station talking and laughing with her sealed the deal for me. I was a goner for this stunning, smart woman.
